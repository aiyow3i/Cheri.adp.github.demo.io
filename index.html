Frequently Asked Questions

We're here to answer any questions you might have

We are still expanding! Try searching nearby or check back later—we're always adding more options. If you have a store in mind, go to Profile > Settings > Recommend a Store.

Store contact info is usually listed on their profile. If not, let us know, and we'll try to help bridge the gap the best we can.

Tap the Discover tab and browse stores sorted by distance. You can also search by name or category. Make sure location services are enabled for the best results.

Stores manage their own hours and inventory through our app. If they're closed or paused orders, check back later or try similar stores nearby.

Stores control their own menus/pricing. If something seems off, refresh the app or contact the store directly—we don't modify their listings.

We connect you with stores that offer delivery, but we don't handle deliveries ourselves. Delivery times and fees depend on the store you choose.

Tracking availability depends on the store. Check your order confirmation for updates or contact the store directly.

Since stores handle deliveries, please contact them directly for order issues. We're happy to help connect you if needed.

Contact the store directly for refund requests. If you need help, our support team can assist in connecting you with the store.
